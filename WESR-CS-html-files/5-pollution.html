 GLOBE at Night

The Globe at Night is an international citizen-science campaign to raise public awareness … of the impact of light pollution on health environment and society. Citizen scientists measure the night sky brightness and submit their observations which can then be accessed on the Globe at Night data base. Over 200,000 measurements from 180 countries have been made in the last 14 years. Observers can use the Loss of the Night app developed by Germany’s Cosalux GmbH to help with their observations . The app is available for android devices from the app store. see more...

Loss of the night app

The Loss of the Night app turns your eyes into a light meter, allowing you to become a … citizen scientist and report how bright the night sky is where you live! You can help monitor skyglow using this app! It's based on Google's Sky Map, and lets you make measurements with a very sensitive, stable, and well understood light meter: your eyes! All you need to do is look for certain stars in the sky, and tell us whether you can see them or not. see more...

AURORA - Tackling Climate Change

Project AURORA is demonstrating how people can make a difference through the choices they make … to reduce the over 30% of carbon emissions linked to residential energy use and transport choices. Citizens can download the AURORA Energy Tracker app from this link. With the app they can measure and bench mark their carbon emissions against their peers using a brand new carbon labelling scheme that tracks performance against the Paris Commission climate change targets. Launched in September 2023 the app now has over 60,000 data points. It is the most comprehensive citizen science project tackling climate change mitiation in Europe. see more...
