Let’s Do It!

Starting in Estonia in 2008, the Let's Do It World Clean Up Day has become the biggest civic movement in human history. … In 2023, 19.1 million participants from 198 Countries and Territories, some 90% of UN Member States collected 218,704 tons of mis-managed waste. In July 2023, the UN recognised 20th September as World Clean Up Day on the UN calendar. You can join and help clean up the planet by downloading the Let's Do It app. see more...
